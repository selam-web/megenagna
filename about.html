About Megenagna Ethiopian Market

Megenagna Ethiopian market located on the corner of Randolph and Veir Mill Rd Silver Spring MD. Our market is serving the community in the greater silver spring area for three years.

Megenaga focuses on providing the surrounding community with fresh quality Ethiopian products. We carry large variety of Ethiopian goods and services from spice, to meats, to traditional clothes to carry out, gift products and much more.
